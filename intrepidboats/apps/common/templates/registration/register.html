WHAT'S THIS?

Hull ID Numbers (HIN) are a 12 character series of letters and numbers affixed to your boat that is used to trace a boat's history. The HIN is required by federal law and uniquely identifies a boat the same way a VIN does with a car. Note, a HIN is not the same as a state registration number which is a boat’s version of a license plate.
